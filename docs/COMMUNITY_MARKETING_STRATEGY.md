# Community & Marketing Strategy

**Timeline:** March 2026 → December 2026 (first deliveries)
**Goal:** Build a qualified email list and engaged community that converts to preorders in September 2026.

---

## The Funnel

```
Awareness (video content, social, word of mouth)
    ↓
Landing page visit
    ↓
Chat with assistant / browse content
    ↓
Email signup (with optional disability profile)
    ↓
Community join (WhatsApp / Telegram)
    ↓
Ongoing engagement (blog posts, video updates, live Q&A)
    ↓
Preorder (September 2026)
    ↓
Delivery (December 2026)
```

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Prototype (Now → September 2026)

### Content Calendar

The content strategy centres on **showing, not telling**. JB uses Le Jibé every day — that's the content.

**Video content (JB provides, we embed and share):**
- Everyday use clips: moving around the house, navigating outdoors, carrying objects
- Close-up of the joystick in action — the "body doesn't move" demonstration
- Curb mounting / step handling — the front wheel advantage
- Getting in and out of the device — transfer demonstration
- Side-by-side: classic wheelchair task vs. same task with Le Jibé

**Blog posts (we write, JB reviews):**
- JB's story: why he built it, what nothing else solved
- The front wheel: why stability changes everything
- The custom seat: why one-size-fits-all is a lie
- The joystick: why body-independent control matters
- "Your wheelchair stays yours" — why Le Jibé isn't a replacement
- Pricing transparency: what €9,800 gets you and why
- Build updates: progress toward the first prototype

**Posting cadence:**
- 1 blog post every 2 weeks
- 1-2 video clips per week shared in community channels
- Monthly live audio Q&A in WhatsApp/Telegram (JB answers questions directly)

### Community Channels

**WhatsApp group (primary):**
- Lower barrier to entry than Telegram for the French disability community
- JB posts updates, photos, short videos directly
- Monthly live audio sessions
- Members can ask questions, share experiences
- Moderated (JB + Richard)

**Telegram channel (secondary):**
- Broadcast-style updates for those who prefer Telegram
- Mirrors key WhatsApp content
- Comments enabled for discussion

**Why not a Facebook group:**
- Can add later, but WhatsApp/Telegram are more intimate and direct
- The disability community in France is active on these platforms
- Easier to manage, less algorithmic noise

### Email Nurture

**Welcome sequence (automated, 3 emails):**
1. Bienvenue — what Le Jibé is, link to JB's story blog post, community group invite
2. Le fauteuil en détail — features deep-dive, video embed, link to chat widget for questions
3. On construit ensemble — prototype timeline, how to follow progress, invite to share with someone who might benefit

**Ongoing newsletter (manual, 2x/month):**
- Build progress updates
- New video content
- Community highlights
- Blog post links
- Countdown to preorder opening

**Personalisation (for users who consented to profile data):**
- Segment by disability type and transfer method
- Tailor messaging: e.g. users who need flat-side seats get content about the custom seat first
- Preorder announcement personalised to their specific situation

## Phase 2: Preorder Launch (September 2026)

### Launch Sequence

**T-2 weeks:** Teaser email — "Bientôt disponible en précommande"
**T-1 week:** Community exclusive — early access link for WhatsApp/Telegram members
**T-0:** Full email list announcement with personalised messaging
**T+1 week:** Blog post — first prototype results, JB's testing feedback
**T+2 weeks:** Follow-up email to non-converters — FAQ, chat widget reminder

### Preorder Messaging

- Frame as an investment, not a purchase: "Investissez dans votre autonomie"
- €9,800 delivered — financing available for those who need it
- Custom-fitted to YOUR body — measurement process explained
- Expected delivery: December 2026
- Your classic wheelchair stays yours — no reimbursement conflict

## Phase 3: Post-Delivery (December 2026+)

### Referral

Word of mouth is the primary growth channel in the disability community. After delivery:
- Ask customers for permission to film a short testimonial
- Share in community channels and on landing page
- Offer referral incentive (discount on accessories, or credit toward future orders)

### Ongoing Content

- Customer stories (video and blog)
- Usage tips and tricks
- Maintenance guides
- Community meetups (if demand exists)

## What We Don't Do

- No paid advertising (yet) — organic and community-first
- No Facebook/Instagram ads — too expensive for a niche product pre-prototype
- No influencer partnerships (yet) — JB IS the influencer, his authenticity is the product's best asset
- No pushy sales copy — the product sells itself to people who need it
