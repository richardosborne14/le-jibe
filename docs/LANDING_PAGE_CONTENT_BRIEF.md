### Problem Section (NEW — not in current template)
This section explains WHY Le Jibé exists by describing what's wrong with every alternative.

**Key points to convey (not as a list — as editorial prose):**
- Classic electric wheelchairs have no front stabilising wheel → user leans forward/back/side → unintended movement → fall risk
- Only one model in France has the front wheel (rare, limited availability), but it has no electric propulsion
- Electric alternatives are twice the price and sacrifice stability for terrain capability
- Seats on existing three-wheel frames use a sloped design that many paraplegic users physically cannot get into or out of
- The weight of three-wheel frames + electric systems makes solo car loading impossible
- Committing to a non-standard frame means 5 years locked in if it doesn't work out (reimbursement cycle)

**Le Jibé's answer:** Keep your classic wheelchair. Use your reimbursement for that. Le Jibé is your second device — the one that transforms your daily life. Custom seat fits YOUR body. Flat sides if you need them. Front wheel for safety. Joystick so your body stays free.

### Features Section
Updated from "detachable base" framing to "complete device" framing.

**Card 1: Base Segway + roue avant stabilisatrice**
- Large all-terrain wheels (gyroscopic stabilisation)
- Front stabilising wheel: reduces fall risk dramatically
- Mounts curbs and small steps effortlessly
- All surfaces: grass, gravel, tile, uneven terrain
- NO speed mention

**Card 2: Siège carbone sur mesure**
- 3D-printed carbon fibre, fitted to the user's exact body dimensions
- Flat open sides OR supported sides — configured to the user's transfer method and trunk stability
- User keeps their own custom cushion — just swap it between chairs
- Light, strong, comfortable for a full day

**Card 3: Joystick & contrôle intuitif**
- Rigid-mount joystick on one side of the frame
- Gentle push tips the frame → Segway moves in that direction
- Body movement does NOT cause displacement — lean forward, turn, carry objects freely
- 360° rotation on the spot — manoeuvrable in the narrowest corridors
- Natural control in minutes
